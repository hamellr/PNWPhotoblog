Overview

The PNWPhotoBlog iPhone App is meant to give travelers important locations "nearby" to check out. The goal is to help people explore interesting new locations that they might not know about.

The map system will use Google Maps API. Locations shown are based on a distance setting, 1 mile, 5 miles, 10 miles, 25 miles, 50 miles. The map can also be browseable so that users can "set" their location (such as a final destination) and see nearby intereting locations.

Additional user features:

  • Bookmarks with alerts: to track locations you'd like to visit and be alerted when you're nearby
  • Facebook Integration: Allows users to share their favorite locations to Facebook
